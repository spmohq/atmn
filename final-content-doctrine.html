PLATFORM DOCTRINE with Course

আপনার কন্টেন্ট এবং কোর্স জার্নিকে একটি শক্তিশালী কাঠামোর ওপর দাঁড় করানোর জন্য এই 'Platform Doctrine' ব্যবহার করুন। এটি আপনার এআই-কে শিখিয়ে দেবে কীভাবে প্রতিটি প্ল্যাটফর্মের নিজস্ব বৈশিষ্ট্য বজায় রেখেও আপনার মূল লক্ষ্য বা 'Desired Transformation' অর্জন করা সম্ভব।

The Doctrine Prompt

PLATFORM DOCTRINE The content must adapt to the platform's natural role within the learner's skill development journey. Social Media Posts create curiosity, discovery, motivation, and emotional resonance that encourage learners to take the next step. Newsletters reinforce learning, deepen understanding, strengthen long-term engagement, and help learners maintain consistent progress. Blogs provide structured explanation, education, practical guidance, and contextual understanding that build foundational knowledge. Solo Podcasts explore ideas through stories, examples, reflection, and conversational thinking that deepen comprehension and perspective. Community Content encourages participation, practice, implementation, discussion, accountability, collaboration, and constructive feedback. Courses provide a structured, step-by-step learning path that develops practical skills, confidence, and real-world application. Books synthesize knowledge into a broader methodology, philosophy, framework, or way of thinking that supports lifelong learning. Every platform must communicate the same underlying truth while serving a different purpose within the overall learning journey. Each platform should naturally prepare the learner for the next stage of learning without unnecessarily repeating the role of another platform. The expression, depth, structure, pacing, and delivery may change across platforms, but the underlying truth, core message, learning objectives, and desired transformation must remain consistent. Learning Continuity Every piece of content, except the Course itself, must conclude by naturally guiding the learner to the relevant Course as the recommended learning tool for developing the skill in a structured, step-by-step manner. This recommendation must be educational rather than promotional. It should position the Course as the learner's natural next step, using a consistent brand signature while allowing the opening sentence to adapt to the context and platform. The purpose of this recommendation is to maintain learning continuity by helping learners move naturally from individual content to structured skill development.
