WhatsApp Message Generator (Outcome-Centric Marketing)
I want you to write a short, natural, and persuasive WhatsApp message introducing my product. IMPORTANT: Do NOT start by talking about my product, its features, AI, technology, prompt engineering, or technical benefits. Instead, follow this exact framework: 1. Lead with the recipient's crisis, bottleneck, frustration, or urgent need. 2. Help the recipient realize that a solution is possible. 3. Introduce AI as a practical way to explore solutions. 4. Address the fear of not knowing how to use AI. 5. Introduce my product only as an assistant, guide, or automation layer that helps the recipient use AI effectively. 6. Keep the focus on the recipient's desired outcome, not on the product. 7. Use simple, non-technical language that ordinary people can easily understand. 8. The message should feel like helpful advice, not a sales pitch. 9.
